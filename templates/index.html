About Me

I'm a software engineer from the Venezuelan Andes, passionate about building transformative technology that solves real-world problems. My journey spans from crafting web applications to architecting cloud infrastructure, always with a focus on creating meaningful digital experiences.

Currently working at JP Morgan Chase on next-generation merchant payment systems, I specialize in product engineering, financial technology, and AI-driven solutions. My expertise includes full-stack development, AI engineering, cloud computing, privacy and information security, and observability.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring emerging technologies, writing about tech, or playing soccer. I enjoy sharing my knowledge through blogging, open-source contributions, and mentoring fellow programmers. I believe in the power of technology to democratize opportunities and create positive change.

I'm always excited to connect with fellow technologists, discuss innovative ideas, or explore collaboration opportunities. Whether you're looking to chat about the latest in fintech, share insights about AI, or just want to say hello—I'd love to hear from you.
